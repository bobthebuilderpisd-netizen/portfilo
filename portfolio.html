I'm Jojoinzoravar — been on Roblox since 2021 and spent that time actually building something. I've worked through staff teams, admin structures, QA pipelines, and department roles across some of the platform's biggest communities.

Most of my work lives at Stateview Prison — the #1 prison roleplay group on Roblox with close to a million members. I currently hold Chief of Staff (CoS) and Events Program Coordinator (EPC) there. I came up through the ranks — started as a Moderator, worked through Community Management, the Intelligence Agency, Events Team, Game Administration, Support Agency, and the Newsletter Team on the way up.

At ImagineXperience I'm an Admin and active Tester — and I was previously on their Board of Directors. At BeneVocon I do QA work. Separately, I held SCA and LCA roles at Nourity.co — a completely different org. I also test and help out players at Double Dimension Studios, and I play Cream.
